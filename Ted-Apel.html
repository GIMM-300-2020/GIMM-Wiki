Ted Apel

Career at GIMM

Ted Apel is a Clinical Associate Professor in the GIMM program. Ted is an audio technology researcher and sound artist working with embedded audio devices and signal processing techniques. He teaches courses in Physical Computing, Interactive Video, Game Design, and Game Audio Programming.

 

Industry Experience

Ted received his BA from Pomona College, MA from Dartmouth College, and his PhD in computer music from the University of California, San Diego. 

 

Interesting Facts

He designed the sound effects of Tickle Me Elmo.
